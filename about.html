Current Projects

Annexe Cafe

Annexe Cafe serves fresh, healthy, and affordable food, open to all, with a three-course lunch deal for vulnerable residents, using surplus supermarket food, and hosts celebratory meals throughout the year.

Participatory Budgeting Initiative

Partick and Thornwood Ideas Fund is a Scottish Government-funded Participatory Budgeting initiative allowing residents to decide on community spending through grants, overseen by Annexe Communities' steering group.

Social Prescribing Project

Social Prescribing project offers personalized pathways for improving health and wellbeing through one-to-one sessions, social interactions at the cafe, and diverse group activities, ideal for enhancing health and increasing social interaction.

Younity Project

Younity Project, funded by The National Lottery Community Fund, aims to connect Partick's local organizations and individuals, sharing resources, knowledge, space, expertise, training, and volunteering opportunities for mutual benefit.
