Preparing for Exhibition Day

  • Dream-Caches

This morning marked a significant milestone for our project as we submitted the poster, meticulously ensuring it effectively conveys our project's core challenges and innovative solutions. To make it visually captivating, we employed color contrast and incorporated icons that resonate with the essence of our work. We also included a QR code in the poster, enabling visitors at tomorrow's exhibition to scan and explore our prototypes virtually. Of course, we will also have our physical prototypes ready for them to experience firsthand.

As the day progressed, we grew more confident about tomorrow's exhibition and are filled with anticipation to share our journey and solutions with the visitors. We can't wait to receive valuable feedback and insights from the attendees, and we are truly excited about the positive impact our project can make in the community.
