Exhibition Day

  • Dream-Caches
  • August 3, 2023

Today was a fun-filled and eventful day as we showcased our project to everyone in the exhibition. This morning, we set up everything we needed, including our pre-prepared physical prototypes, ensuring that we were ready to present our solutions.



During the exhibition, many individuals approached our booth, eager to learn more about our project and how our solutions addressed the challenges. It was an unforgettable experience to share our journey and see the genuine interest and engagement from the attendees. The positive feedback we received was truly encouraging, with many expressing their appreciation for the uniqueness and effectiveness of our solutions.

Jane, our client, was present at the exhibition as well. We had a great time discussing our project with her and took pictures together to record the moment. She was so impressed with our poster that we happily agreed to offer her one in thanks for her support throughout the project.



Apart from showcasing our project, we also took the opportunity to visit other teams’ booths to learn about their projects and solutions. It was a valuable experience to see the diverse range of ideas that our classmates had come up with.

Overall, today’s exhibition was a pleasant experience for us. It served as a great closure to our project, and we are now focused on finalising our static website by updating our dailies, including today’s and tomorrow’s. As we near the end of this journey, we are proud of what we have accomplished and excited to see how our initiative will affect the community.
